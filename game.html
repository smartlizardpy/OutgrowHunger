Outgrow Hunger

Manage resources, build trust, and survive the season.

Standard resources. Standard challenge.
Objective: Survive 30 Days.
On Day 30, you must have:
Hunger < 10%  |  Resilience > 80%

How to Play

1. The Daily Loop

Each day brings a random Event. Choose wisely! Your choices affect your stats and resources.

2. Manage Needs

After the event, use your Action Bar to eat (Bread) or drink (Water) if your stats are critical.

3. End the Day

Click "End Day" to sleep.
Warning: Hunger rises and Trust falls each night (amount depends on difficulty and your trust momentum).

4. Win Conditions

On Day 30, you must have:
- Hunger below 10%
- Resilience above 80%

5. Losing

You lose immediately if Hunger hits 100%, Resilience hits 0%, or Trust hits 0%.
