Why We Built Outgrow Hunger

Outgrow Hunger is a short survival narrative designed to make food insecurity, trust, and resilience feel personal and urgent.

Our Goal

We wanted players to experience hard trade-offs: when to spend, when to share, and when to protect themselves. The game is about systems, but also about people.

Why This Matters

Hunger is not just about missing a meal. It affects health, education, social trust, and long-term opportunity. Through play, we hope to turn statistics into empathy and action.
